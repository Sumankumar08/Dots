HOW TO PLAY

Everyone as a child would have definitely played this game in school. The game is very simple.

Connect the dots to make a box. The player gets a turn to connect two dots. And the next turn would be played by the next player. Once the square is complete you get that box as 1 point. This game is a 6x6 grid. Once the whole grid is complete the player with the highest number of boxes wins.

No diagonal lines are allowed in the game. Only 1 chance per turn.

Have fun and enjoy the game!!
